Meet the Artist

Lloyd Hamrol, a UCLA graduate born in 1937 is the artist responsible for many works including the Log Ramps here on the WWU campus! He started out teaching at several differnt Universities in California until 1979 when he decided to become a full time sculptor. He is now known for his physically engaging public art pieces that inspire adventure in an outdoor space.
